Membership & Annual Events

🎉 New Year Celebration (January)

Start the year with music, cultural performances, and community dinner bringing families together.

🍲 Adhoc Potluck (Spring)

Casual meetups where families bring homemade food, share stories, and enjoy community bonding.

📚 Saraswati Puja (February)

Celebrate knowledge, learning, and arts with traditional rituals and cultural programs for children.

🪔 Durga Puja (Fall)

The grand annual festival featuring rituals, cultural performances, food stalls, and large gatherings.

🦃 Thanksgiving Gathering (November)

A warm cultural blend of gratitude celebration with Bengali food, music, and community dinner.
